About Reports

When a learner takes the courses offered by Learn Education, it is guaranteed that he or she acquire high standards of knowledge. However, it is important that the same is substantiated by a report. Hence, the report section offers comprehensive reports that can underline the core strengths and also areas that need further attention.

Parents and teachers can use the reports creatively to define short term and long term goal in quantitative or qualitative parameters. The gap between expectation and delivery can be minimized by concentrating on the areas that need improvement. Statistical reports tell about the deviation in the performance in different areas for improvement.

How are these reports helpful?

Teachers and parents can make use of these reports to track chapter wise performance. Various statistical models can be built on the data. It helps in understanding the trouble spots precisely and pin-pointedly. There are reports to know about the average login time, assignment submission status, and overall performance.

Learn Education believes in the concept of positive motivation. Learners are awarded Gold, Silver or Bronze medals based on the predefined eligibility criteria defined by experts. These medals encourage the learners to achieve further milestones by raising the bar higher.
